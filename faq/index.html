FAQ

Common questions, answered.

If your question isn't here, book a 15-minute call. No upsell games.

Do I really get my site in 5 days?
Yes. From the moment you complete intake, your site is live within 5 business days (Starter), 10 days (Pro), or 14 days (Premium). We're on time or you get 10% off — automatically.
What if I don't like it?
You don't pay. Full refund, no questions, no fine print. We work until you love it. If you still don't, your money comes back.
Do I need to provide content?
You give us your basics — business info, photos if you have them, and what makes you different. We handle the rest. Pro and Premium include professional copywriting so you don't have to write a word.
Do I have to host with you?
No. Hosting is optional ($49–$149/month). But we strongly recommend it — without it, your site is your problem when it breaks. With it, it's our problem and we handle updates, security, and backups.
What industries do you work with?
Plumbers, HVAC, roofers, landscapers, electricians, auto repair, salons, food trucks, restaurants, fitness studios, dog groomers, contractors — pretty much any small business that needs to be found online. We probably know your industry.
Can you redesign my existing site?
Yes. Same price. We start fresh — it's faster, the result is better, and we don't inherit anyone else's tech debt.
Are you a one-person operation?
Yes — for now. That's why we're affordable and fast. You work directly with the person building your site, not a project manager handing it off to a freelancer in another country.
