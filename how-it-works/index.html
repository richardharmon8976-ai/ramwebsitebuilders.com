Timeline

What the 5 days actually look like.

For Starter. Pro and Premium follow the same pattern stretched to 10 and 14 days.

Day 0

You order online

Order confirms. You get an intake link within minutes.

Day 1

Intake + kickoff

You complete the 10-min intake. We confirm scope and start building the same day.

Day 2–3

Design + build

Mobile-first layout, copy, on-page SEO, contact form, hosting setup. You get a working draft on Day 3.

Day 4

Revisions

You review. We tighten copy, swap photos, adjust layout. One revision round is standard.

Day 5

Launch

Your site goes live on your domain with SSL and analytics. You start getting calls.

What we need from you

  • Your business name, phone, address, and hours
  • A short paragraph on what you do and who you serve
  • Logo if you have one — we'll refresh it if not
  • 5–10 photos of your work, your team, or your shop
  • Reviews from Google, Yelp, or Facebook to feature

What you get at each step

  • Day 1: Confirmation email + scope summary
  • Day 3: Live preview link of the draft site
  • Day 4: Revised site + final review checklist
  • Day 5: Live site on your domain + admin handoff
  • Day 6+: Optional monthly hosting & maintenance
