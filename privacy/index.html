When you fill in a form on this site (booking a call or joining the email list), we collect the information you type in — typically your email address, and anything else you choose to share. We use your email to reply to your inquiry and, if you joined the list, to occasionally send tips and offers. Every list email includes a way to unsubscribe. If you ask us to delete your information, we will — email us and it's done.
